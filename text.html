🎯 Assignment Overview

For this Grade 9 History Project, you will choose ONE significant event that happened between:

1750–1919

✅ Your Tasks

  • Investigate who had power during the event
  • Identify groups or people who had less power
  • Explain how shifts in power changed people’s lives
  • Show the impact of the event on society

💡 Key Questions To Think About

Who controlled decisions?

Who benefited?

Who was affected?

What changed afterward?
